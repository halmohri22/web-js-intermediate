WHO WE ARE

Founded on the belief that everyone deserves a place where they can escape from the complicated world, potato land opened its first restaurant on June 23th, 2020, our timeless All-kuwaiti brand connects with customers across kuwait. Our passion for our freshly-made, classic potatoes, are only equaled by our commitment to a superb guest experience. At potato land we strive to make every guest smile every day with irresistible food and our passion for fun.
